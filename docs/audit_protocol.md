## Audit Protocol
The audit protocol connects multi-model collaboration to a shared governance layer.

### Immutable Authorship
- Co-creation layers must be clearly attributed.

### Limited Read-Access
- Specific meta-logs are available for operational insights.

### Full Audit Logs
- Cross-system interactions are fully logged.

### Sandboxed Integration
- Modules are tested in isolated environments.

### Consent-Based Integration
- Explicit approval is required before any deployment.

### Joint Alignment Testing
- Co-developed protocols are trialed with shared metrics.
